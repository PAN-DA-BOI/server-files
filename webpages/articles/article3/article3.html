Food World

Food World as a company went out of buisness in 2017, but instead of outright closing its stores, it intigrated them into other chains. In our case, we found evidence that this one was open up into 2021, when we assume COVID killed off business

Back to Articles

The building was originally built in 1966 with a square footage of 21,290 FT2, the lot size is 1.79 Acres. It had a bakery, pizza oven, and lots of the cool features older stores had before going corporate and "official". Truly a time to be alive.

In source 2, page 3, you can see the latest plans of turning the building into condos/residential spaces.
