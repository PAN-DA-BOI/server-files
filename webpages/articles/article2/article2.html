Police Training House

The house after its being abandoned fell into the hands of the Woods Cross city. it couldnt be sold becuase of the asbestos everywhere, so was used for police training till its demolition in 2025

This villa house was Built in 1976. the property mesures 16552.8 Square feet, and the 3048 square foot single family home has 4 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms.

the property has been demolished as per page 9 of the February 4th 2025 woods cross City Council minutes noted at "CONSIDERATION TO ADOPT RESOLUTION 2025-891 APPROVING THE CONTRACT FOR THE BUILDING DEMOLITION AND PROPERTY CLEANUP AT 1351 W 1500 S"
